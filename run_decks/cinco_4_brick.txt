Cinco 4 Brick Model with a planar switch
!
! Rick Spielman 2020-04-13
!
Time-step 1e-12
Resolution-time 1e-9
End-time 1.5e-6
Number-prints 5
Execute-cycles all
Grids no
Echo-setup no
Max-points 20001
!
!Start circuit definition
!
! Capacitance for a single brick is 2x140 nF = 280 nF
! The ESR for a single brick is 0.5 x 0.130 = 0.065 ½ (65 m½)
! The base brick inductance is 90 nH.
! The matched resistance is ~ 0.61 ½.
!
! Capacitance for four bricks is 4x280 nF = 1120 nF
! ESR = 0.065/4 = 0.01625 ½
! Inductance is 90 nH/4 - 22.5 nH
! Load inductance = 6 nH
! Rmatched = 0.16 ½
! N switch = 4
!
! We will use the Martin Switch model for the planar switch
!
! The charge voltage will be 100 kV. This is the maximum voltage.
!
!
BRANCH
!
! Capacitor capacitance and charge voltage
!
RCGround 1e+12 1120.0e-9
Initial VC1 100e3
TXT VC1
$V_brick
TXT EC1
$E_brick
!
! Cap inductance, case & parasitic inductance, and ESR
!
RLSeries 0.01625 20e-9
TXT ER2
$E_ESR
!
! Switch resistance - Martin Model and switch inductance - 
!
! 5 channels in parallel, total switch gap is 0.6 cm x 7 (# gaps) = 4.2 cm = 0.042 m 
!
RLSeries 10e9 2.5e-9
Var R2 Switch
!dielectric switchtime gap              pressure     nswitch nchannels
AIR           0.0               0.042          1.0                4            5
TXT R2
$R_switch (½)
TXT ER2
$E_switch (J)
TXT FCH
$Radius_switch (m)

!
! Output TL header inducatance
!
RLSeries 0.0 6.0e-9
!
! Load resistance = Rmatched
!
RCGround 0.08 0.0
TXT IR1 SCAle 1e-3
$I_brick (kA)
TXT QR1
$Q_brick
TXT VR1
$V_out
TXT PR1
$P_out
TXT ER1
$E_out
!
! End circuit
!
