Simple Opening Switch Calculations
!
TIME-STEP          10E-12
RESOLUTION-TIME    20E-12
END-TIME           50E-9
NUMBER-PRINTS      5
EXECUTE-CYCLES     ALL
Grids              NO
ECHO               NO	
MAX-POINTS         4001
!
!
!   Rick Spielman
!
!  A model of an idealized opening switch looking at losses in the switch and
!  effects of the load capacitance and resistance.
!

BRANCH
VOLTsource TAB 1 0
! SF     t-delay
100      0
!time    voltage
0.0      1
1.0e-09  1
50e-09   1
LASt-entry

!
! The storage inductor
!

RLSeries 0.0 2e-6
INITial IL2 100

!
! The RCG acting as the variable resistance for the switch
!

RCG 1e12 0.0
VARiable R1 TABle
1.0 0.0
0 0.0
1e-09 0.0
2e-09 1e6
50e-09 1e6
LASt-entry

!
! Parasitic series L
!

RLSeries 0 10e-09

!
! Load Resistance + Parasitic Capacitance
!

RCG 500.0 5e-12
UFO IR1
$I_Load
UFO VR1
$V_Load

!end Main branch
