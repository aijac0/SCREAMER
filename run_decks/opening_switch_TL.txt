TL Opening Switch Calculations

!
!  A model of an idealized opening switch looking at losses in the switch and
!  effects of the load capacitance and resistance. Use a TL as the storage inductor.
!

BRANCH
!
! The storage TL
!

TRLine LINear 15e-9 200
INItial ITRl 100

!
! The RCG acting as the variable resistance for the switch
!

RCG 1e12 0.0
VARiable R1 TABle
1.0 0.0
0 0.0
1e-09 0.0
2e-09 1e6
50e-09 1e6
LASt-entry
UFO IR1
$I_switch
UFO VR1
$V_switch
!
! Parasitic series L
!

RLSeries 0 10e-09

!
! Load Resistance + Parasitic Capacitance
!

RCG 100.0 5e-12
UFO IR1
$I_Load
UFO VR1
$V_Load

!end Main branch
